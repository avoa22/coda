About KathuCoda

KathuCoda is a Music School founded by the seasoned musicians - Stanley Bros. John and Christopher — bring over 20 years of international experience in live performances and global music education.

Coming from a rich musical background, both have mentored students across age groups and skill levels. Christopher is also a playback singer who has collaborated with leading industry names including Ilaiyaraaja, Harris Jayaraj, Vijay Antony, Parthiban, and Prabhu Deva.

Launched in 2024, KathuCoda was built to redefine music learning for the Gen Z generation — blending the depth of traditional methods with a modern, simplified approach that makes music more accessible and engaging to all. In just two years, KathuCoda has inspired nearly 150+ students, ranging from ages 5 to 70 years, helping them rediscover and grow in their musical journey.

The journey is just getting started — and only going upward.
